# TicketNetwork - Checkout & Payment Reference

## Overview
TicketNetwork (https://www.ticketnetwork.com) is a secondary ticket marketplace where users can purchase tickets from other sellers. The checkout process requires a registered account and billing information.

## Site Structure
- **Homepage**: https://www.ticketnetwork.com
- **Primary Categories**: Sports, Concerts, Theater
- **Secondary Categories**: Pop, Rock, Country, Hip-Hop, Festivals, Latin, Comedy, Broadway, Las Vegas, NFL, NBA, NHL, MLS

## Ticket Search and Selection
- Users search for events by team/artist/event name/venue
- Event listings show:
  - Event name
  - Date and time
  - Venue location
  - Available tickets with prices (sorted by price when applicable)
  - Seller information
  - Ticket condition/details

## Ticket Pricing
- Displayed as total price (includes all fees)
- Prices are market-determined (may be above or below face value)
- No hidden fees policy ("Buy & Sell Tickets to Concerts, Sports & Theater Events - No Hidden Fees")
- Price locking mechanism with timer (approximately 10 minutes) - cart hold timer for time-sensitive tickets

## Checkout Flow
1. **Event Selection**: Browse and select event
2. **Ticket Selection**: Choose specific tickets from available listings
3. **Add to Cart**: Ticket held in cart for ~10 minutes
4. **Checkout Page**: Enter delivery and billing information
5. **Payment**: Complete payment to finalize purchase

## Checkout Form Fields
### Delivery Information
- **Note**: TicketNetwork is a digital ticket marketplace
- **Billing Address Only** (no shipping address required):
  - First Name
  - Last Name
  - Address (Street)
  - City
  - State
  - ZIP/Postal Code
  - Phone Number
  - Email Address

### Billing Information
- Credit card details (see payment methods below)
- Billing address (may default to same as above)

## Cart Behavior
- **Ticket Hold Timer**: Approximately 10 minutes
- If cart expires: Select another available ticket for the same event
- Cart shows item count and total price

## Account Requirements
- Account registration required before purchase
- Email verification needed after account creation
- Save information for faster checkouts option available
- Ability to track orders and access event details from account

## Important Transaction Details
- **100% Money-Back Guarantee**: Advertised on site footer
- **Verification**: Email verification required for account activation
- **Order Tracking**: Users can track order status and access delivery information
- **Event Details**: Account holders can access and customize event information
