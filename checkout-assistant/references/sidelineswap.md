# SidelineSwap Checkout Guide

## Overview
SidelineSwap checkout is a secure process that guides users through shipping address entry and payment. Products can be purchased via "Buy Now" listings or through "Make Offer" (auction-style).

## Finding Products

### Browsing
- Navigate to category pages (Hockey, Baseball, etc.) or use search
- Filter by price, condition, brand, and other attributes
- Look for "Buy Now" fixed-price listings rather than "Best Offer" auctions

### Identifying Buy Now Listings
- "Buy Now" listings show a fixed price displayed prominently
- Often have clearance badges (e.g., "Spring Sale: 15% Off")
- Include estimated delivery dates
- Show seller information and ratings

### Example Product Details
- Product name displayed prominently
- Original price and sale price (if applicable)
- "Buy Now" button (green button for fixed-price purchases)
- "Add to Cart" button alternative
- Seller information with feedback rating
- Estimated delivery timeframe (e.g., "Fri, Apr 10 - Tue, Apr 14")

## Checkout Flow

### Step 1: Select Product
1. Click on a product listing
2. Review product details, pricing, and seller information
3. Click "Buy Now" to proceed to checkout

### Step 2: Shipping Address Form
The checkout page displays a "Shipping" section with required fields:

**Contact Information:**
- Email (pre-filled with logged-in user's email)

**Address Fields:**
- Country (dropdown, default: United States)
- First Name (required)
- Last Name (required)
- Address 1 (required) - Street address
- Address 2 (optional) - Apartment, suite, etc.
- City (required)
- State (required, dropdown selection)
- Postal Code/Zip Code (required)

**Important Notes:**
- The form uses Google Maps address autocomplete for Address 1 field
- Once a valid address is entered, the form will suggest matching addresses
- All required fields must be completed to continue

### Step 3: Review Order
On the right side of the form:
- Product image and name
- Original price and discounted price
- Spring Sale discount (if applicable)
- Shipping cost (calculated after continuing)
- Taxes (calculated after continuing)
- Total price (updated with shipping/taxes)
- "Complete Purchase" button (appears to be for next step)

### Step 4: Continue to Payment
- Click the "Continue" button to proceed past the shipping form
- This takes you to the payment information page
- Payment step shows payment method selection

## Payment Methods

### Available Options
- Buyer protection with Buyer Guarantee
- Worry-free returns
- "Buy now, pay later" with Sezzle

### Important
- DO NOT enter actual credit card information for testing
- The checkout page is secure (SSL/TLS encrypted)

## Key Marketplace Features

### Buyer Protection
- All purchases are protected by SidelineSwap Buyer Protection
- Worry-free returns available
- Buy now, pay later option with Sezzle

### Seller Information
- Seller name and rating displayed
- Positive feedback percentage shown (e.g., "99.2% Positive Feedback")
- Shipping origin displayed (e.g., "Ships from: IL, USA")
- Last active status shown (e.g., "Last active: about 9 hours ago")

### Follow & Message Options
- "Follow" button to track seller
- "Message Seller" button for inquiries

## Checkout Tips
- Have shipping address ready before checkout
- Verify all address details match expected location
- Check estimated delivery date matches needs
- Review total price including shipping and any applicable taxes
- Note that Sezzle offers installment payment plans

## Common Issues
- Address autocomplete may require selecting from dropdown suggestions
- State/province field is a dropdown and may require partial text entry
- Form requires all mandatory fields to be completed before proceeding
