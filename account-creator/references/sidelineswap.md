# SidelineSwap Account Creation Guide

## Overview
SidelineSwap is a peer-to-peer marketplace for buying and selling used and new sporting goods. Account creation is straightforward with email/password authentication.

## Signup Process

### Step 1: Navigate to Signup
- Go to https://sidelineswap.com
- Click the "Join" button in the top right navigation bar
- A modal dialog will appear with signup options

### Step 2: Choose Authentication Method
The signup modal offers three options:
- Continue with Google
- Continue with Apple
- Continue with Email

For email-based signup, select "Continue with Email"

### Step 3: Email Signup Form
The email signup form requires:
- **Email**: Account email address
- **Username**: Username for the account
- **Password**: Password for account access

Example values:
- Email: garysmod@agentmail.to
- Username: garychao
- Password: AgentShop2026!

### Step 4: Accept Terms
The signup form includes a checkbox to agree to SidelineSwap Terms of Service. Users must accept these terms to proceed.

### Step 5: Submit Signup
Click the "Sign Up" button to create the account.

## Post-Signup
After successful signup, the user is logged in and can:
- Browse and search for sporting goods
- Add items to cart
- Access their account via the profile menu in the top right
- See "Favorites," "Inbox," and "Cart" icons in navigation when logged in

## Key Features
- Buyer protection with Buyer Guarantee
- Worry-free returns available
- Buy now, pay later with Sezzle
- User ratings and seller feedback visible on product pages
- Estimated delivery dates provided at checkout

## Notes
- Email verification may be required via confirmation link
- Username must be unique across the platform
- Account recovery is available via email
