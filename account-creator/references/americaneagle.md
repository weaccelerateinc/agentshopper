## Merchant Information
- **Merchant**: American Eagle Outfitters (ae.com)
- **URL**: https://www.ae.com/us/en

## Account Creation Flow

### Step 1: Navigate to Signup
- Visit https://www.ae.com/us/en
- Click on account/profile icon in top navigation
- Select "Create an Account" option

### Step 2: Form Fields
The signup form includes the following fields:

#### Required Fields:
1. **Email**: Test with account email (e.g., garysmod@agentmail.to)
2. **First Name**: Text field
3. **Last Name**: Text field
4. **Phone Number**: Format: 10-digit US number (e.g., 6263211250)
5. **Password**: Must meet security requirements
   - Suggested test password: AgentShop2026!
   - Must contain uppercase, lowercase, number, special character
6. **Zip Code**: 5-digit US zip code (e.g., 91770)

#### Additional Options:
- **Terms & Conditions Checkbox**: REQUIRED - Must be checked before form submission
- **Real Rewards Loyalty Program**: Optional enrollment during signup

### Step 3: Form Submission
1. Ensure all required fields are filled with valid data
2. Check the Terms & Conditions checkbox (green checkmark required)
3. Click "Create Account" button
4. Form validates in real-time with green checkmark indicators

### Key Observations:
- Form validation provides immediate visual feedback (green checkmarks)
- Real Rewards credit card offer appears during signup
- Terms & Conditions checkbox must be explicitly checked - form will not submit without it
- Phone number formatting is flexible but must be 10 digits
- Password requirements are strict and indicate strength in real-time
